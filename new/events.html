Events and Competitions

    We participate in many events and competitions in our region. We also host KPMT; more details at the KPMT page.

    American Mathematics Competition (AMC) 10/12
    We host the AMC 10/12 tests at Newport High Schools on February 7 (A) and February 13 (B).

    American Invitational Math Examination
    Those who score exceptionally on the AMC will be invited to take the AIME on a to be determined date.

    Math is Cool
    MIC is a math contest for grade levels 4-12. Competitors compete at a local level for a chance to go to state.
